<p><p>Smart window film market includes two main types: self-adhesive film and non-adhesive film. Self-adhesive film has a sticky backing that allows it to easily adhere to windows, making it a popular choice for easy installation and removal. On the other hand, non-adhesive film relies on static cling to stick to windows without leaving residue, providing a temporary solution for privacy or security. Both types offer benefits such as energy efficiency, UV protection, and customizable privacy options for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
