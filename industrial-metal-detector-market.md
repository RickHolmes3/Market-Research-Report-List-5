<p><p>Industrial metal detector market types include rectangular aperture metal detectors, gravity fall metal detectors, pipeline liquid, paste, and slurry metal detectors, and metal detectors with conveyor systems. Rectangular aperture metal detectors are used for bulk materials, while gravity fall metal detectors are ideal for free-falling products. Pipeline liquid, paste, and slurry metal detectors are designed for detecting metal contaminants in liquid products. Metal detectors with conveyor systems are commonly used for packaged products moving on a conveyor belt for efficient detection and removal of metal contaminants.</p></p>
