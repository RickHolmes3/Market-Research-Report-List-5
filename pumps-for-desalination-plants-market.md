<p><p>Pumps for desalination plants are essential for the efficient operation of the plants. The market for these pumps can be categorized into two main types: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps are commonly used for high flow rates and low-pressure applications, while positive displacement pumps are better suited for high-pressure applications and more viscous liquids. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in ensuring the success and effectiveness of desalination plants.</p></p>
