<p><p>The Construction Glass Curtain Wall Market includes two main types - Unitised Type and Stick Built Type. Unitised Type curtain walls are pre-fabricated in controlled factory conditions and then assembled on-site, offering faster installation and higher quality control. Stick Built Type curtain walls are installed piece by piece on-site, allowing for greater customization but taking longer to install. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on project requirements, budget, and timeline.</p></p>
