<p><p>Biopsy instruments market consists of three main types: Core Needle Biopsy, Aspiration Biopsy, and Vacuum Assisted Biopsy. Core Needle Biopsy involves extracting a small core of tissue using a hollow needle. Aspiration Biopsy uses a thin needle attached to a syringe to extract fluid or cells. Vacuum Assisted Biopsy utilizes a vacuum powered device to collect larger tissue samples quickly and accurately. Each type of biopsy instrument serves a specific purpose in diagnosing and treating various medical conditions.</p></p>
