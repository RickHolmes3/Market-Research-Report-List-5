<p><p>The commercial fresh freezer market can be categorized into two main types: air cooling and direct cold. Air cooling freezers use circulating air to maintain a consistent temperature inside the freezer, ensuring even distribution of cold air. On the other hand, direct cold freezers utilize a system where the cooling element comes in direct contact with the products, allowing for faster and more efficient cooling. Both types of freezers are commonly used in commercial settings to preserve and store fresh food items.</p></p>
