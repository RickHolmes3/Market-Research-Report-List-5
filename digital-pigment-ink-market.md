<p><p>Digital pigment ink market types include Micron Ink and Nanoscale Ink. Micron Ink, as the name suggests, is characterized by particles that are a few micrometers in size, providing a smooth finish and high resolution. Nanoscale Ink, on the other hand, consists of particles that are measured in nanometers, offering even higher resolution and color accuracy. Both types are widely used in the digital printing industry for their ability to produce vivid colors and sharp details in various applications.</p></p>
