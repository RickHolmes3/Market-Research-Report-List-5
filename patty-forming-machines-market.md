<p><p>Patty forming machines are commonly classified into two types: Semi-Automatic and Fully Automatic. Semi-Automatic machines require manual loading of the ingredients and operation of the machine, while the shaping and forming process is automated. In contrast, Fully Automatic machines automate the entire process from ingredient loading to shaping and forming without the need for manual intervention. Both types cater to different production scales and efficiency needs, offering flexibility and convenience in patty production for various food establishments.</p></p>
