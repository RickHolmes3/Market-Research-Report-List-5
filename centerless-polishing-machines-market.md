<p><p>Centerless polishing machines come in two main types: Single Head and Multi Head. Single Head machines are designed for simpler operations and are ideal for small-scale polishing tasks. They are more cost-effective and easier to operate, making them suitable for applications with lower production volumes. These machines are perfect for high-volume production and larger-scale polishing operations.</p></p>
