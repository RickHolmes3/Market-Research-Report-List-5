Desi gram flour is made from a variety of chickpeas that are smaller and darker in color, providing a nuttier flavor to dishes. Kabuli gram flour is made from larger, lighter-colored chickpeas, offering a milder taste. Both types of gram flour are popular choices for making dishes such as pakoras, bhajis, and flatbreads.</p></p>
