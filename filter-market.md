<p><p>Filter market types include Lowpass Filters, which allow low frequencies to pass through while attenuating high frequencies; Highpass Filters, which do the opposite by allowing high frequencies to pass through and attenuating low frequencies; Bandpass Filters, which only allow a specific range of frequencies to pass through while attenuating others; and Others, which includes various specialized filters such as notch filters and all-pass filters. Each filter type serves a specific purpose in signal processing and communication systems, catering to different frequency ranges and applications.</p></p>
