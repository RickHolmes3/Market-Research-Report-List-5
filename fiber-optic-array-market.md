They are commonly used in applications requiring point-to-point connections.</p><p>2D Fiber Arrays consist of fibers arranged in a two-dimensional grid pattern, allowing for more complex connections and greater flexibility in routing signals. They are often used in applications requiring multiple input and output connections.</p></p>
