<p><p>Anesthesia airway management devices can be categorized into two types: basic and advanced. On the other hand, advanced devices are more complex and technologically advanced, including endotracheal tubes, video laryngoscopes, and supraglottic airway devices. These advanced devices may offer better visualization and control of the airway, making them suitable for more challenging airway management situations.</p></p>
