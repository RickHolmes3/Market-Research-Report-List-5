<p><p>Low pressure reverse osmosis membrane market includes two main types - composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes are made up of multiple layers with each layer serving a specific function, such as rejection of salts or other contaminants. Asymmetric membranes have a dense top layer that helps in rejection of contaminants and a porous support layer for structural integrity. Both types of membranes are used in various industries for water purification, wastewater treatment, and desalination processes due to their high efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
