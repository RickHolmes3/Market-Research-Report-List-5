<p><p>Smart Air Cleaners come in different types to cater to varying needs. HEPA filters are highly efficient at removing particles as small as 0.3 microns. Electrostatic precipitators use an electric charge to trap particles in the air. Ionizers release negative ions to attract and remove airborne pollutants. Ozone generators release ozone molecules to neutralize odors and kill bacteria. Each type has its own unique features and benefits, allowing consumers to choose the best option based on their specific air cleaning needs.</p></p>
