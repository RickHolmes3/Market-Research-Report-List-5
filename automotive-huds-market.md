<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive HUDs are categorized into two main types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUDs display information directly onto the windshield, allowing the driver to keep their eyes on the road. Combiner Projected HUDs use a separate transparent display which reflects information onto the driver's line of sight. Both types of HUDs provide essential information such as speed, navigation, and warnings, enhancing the driving experience and safety. These technologies continue to evolve and become more common in modern vehicles.</p></p>
