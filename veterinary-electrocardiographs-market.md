<p><p>Veterinary electrocardiographs are used to monitor the heart's electrical activity in animals. Multiple channels electrocardiographs have the ability to simultaneously record and display different leads of the heart's electrical signals, providing a more comprehensive view of cardiac function. While multiple channels offer a more detailed analysis of cardiac activity, single-channel devices are typically more compact and portable, making them convenient for use in diverse veterinary settings.</p></p>
