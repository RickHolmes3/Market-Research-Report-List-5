<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Tire (IC) Market includes two main types: Bias Tire and Radial Tire. Bias tires are constructed with multiple layers of fabric cords crisscrossed at an angle, providing high stability and durability. Radial tires have fabric layers arranged at 90 degrees from the direction of travel, offering better traction and longer wear life. Both types cater to specific industrial applications, with bias tires being more suitable for heavy-duty use and radial tires preferred for applications requiring superior performance and efficiency.</p></p>
