<p><p>Lead acid batteries for motorcycle market consists of two main types: VRLA batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) batteries are maintenance-free and sealed, making them ideal for motorcycles as they don't require regular topping up with water. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require periodic maintenance and topping up with distilled water. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in the motorcycle industry, catering to the different needs and preferences of riders.</p></p>
