<p><ul><li>Thickness Monitor</li><li>Spectrum Ellipsometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Film Thickness Measuring System market is segmented into two main types - Thickness Monitor and Spectrum Ellipsometer. Thickness Monitor systems are used for real-time monitoring of film thickness during deposition processes, offering accurate and reliable measurements. On the other hand, Spectrum Ellipsometer systems utilize polarized light to measure film thickness and optical constants, providing detailed information about thin film properties. Both types of systems play a crucial role in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, optical coatings, and thin film research.</p></p>
