<p><p>The specialty glass coating market consists of various types such as Pyrolytic Coating, Sputtered Coating, Screen Coating, Spray Coating, and Others. Pyrolytic coating involves applying a thin film through a high-temperature chemical reaction, while Sputtered coating is created by depositing layers of material onto the glass surface through a vacuum process. Screen coating involves using a screen printing method to apply the coating, and Spray coating is applied through a spraying technique. Other types may include innovative or specialized coating methods not covered by the main categories.</p></p>
