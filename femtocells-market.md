<p><p>Femtocells and picocells fall under the category of small cell technology for enhancing cellular coverage and capacity in indoor and outdoor areas. Picocells are larger in coverage compared to femtocells, typically serving a few hundred users in a larger area. Femtocells, on the other hand, are designed for personal or small business use, providing coverage for a limited number of users in a more localized setting such as homes or small offices. Both technologies play a crucial role in improving cellular network performance and expanding coverage.</p></p>
