<p><p>The passenger car tachograph market consists of two main types: integrated and portable. Integrated tachographs are built into the vehicle's dashboard and record driving data directly onto the vehicle's system. Portable tachographs, on the other hand, are detachable devices that can be easily installed and removed from different vehicles. Integrated tachographs are typically more reliable and durable, while portable tachographs offer greater flexibility and convenience for drivers who switch vehicles frequently. Both types play a crucial role in monitoring and ensuring compliance with driving regulations.</p></p>
