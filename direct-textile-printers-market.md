<p><p>Direct textile printers are categorized into two main market types: direct-to-fabric and direct-to-garments. Direct-to-fabric printing involves printing designs directly onto fabric, allowing for customization and vibrant colors. This method is commonly used in home textiles, apparel, and signage industries. Direct-to-garments printing, on the other hand, involves printing designs directly onto finished garments, offering high-quality and durable prints. This method is often used in the fashion and apparel industry for customized and on-demand printing.</p></p>
