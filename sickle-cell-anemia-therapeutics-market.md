<p><p>Sickle Cell Anemia Therapeutics Market includes three main types of treatments- Blood Transfusion, Pharmacotherapy, and Bone Marrow Transplant. Blood transfusion involves the transfer of healthy red blood cells to replace the sickle-shaped cells. Pharmacotherapy includes the use of medication to manage symptoms and complications of the disease. Bone Marrow Transplant is a potentially curative treatment where diseased bone marrow is replaced with healthy bone marrow from a compatible donor. These treatments aim to improve quality of life and manage the symptoms of sickle cell anemia.</p></p>
