<p><p>Closed loop current sensors are used in various applications to accurately measure current flow. Isolated current sensors have a built-in isolation barrier between the current-carrying conductor and the sensing element, providing electrical separation for safety and noise reduction. Non-isolated current sensors do not have this isolation barrier, making them more cost-effective and compact. Isolated sensors are commonly used in industrial and high-voltage applications where safety is a priority, while non-isolated sensors are favored in lower voltage and consumer electronics applications.</p></p>
