<p><p>Construction laminated glass is a type of safety glass that consists of two or more layers of glass bonded together with an interlayer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) or ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA). This interlayer holds the glass together in the event of breakage, providing enhanced safety and security. Construction laminated glass is commonly used in buildings, bridges, facades, and skylights due to its strength, durability, and safety features.</p><p>The Construction Laminated Glass Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.4% during the forecast period. PVB laminated glass is known for its flexibility and impact resistance, while EVA laminated glass offers improved clarity and UV resistance.
