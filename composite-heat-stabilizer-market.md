Metal soap stabilizers are derived from metals like lead, calcium, and zinc, and are effective in PVC applications. Organic heat stabilizers, on the other hand, are made from various organic compounds and are preferred for applications requiring non-toxic alternatives. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the structural integrity of polymers in various industries.</p></p>
