<p><p>Plating on Plastics (POP) involves depositing a layer of metal onto plastic surfaces to enhance their appearance and durability. There are three main types of POP markets: Chrome-based, Nickel-based, and Other Metal-based. Chrome-based plating offers a high level of reflectivity and corrosion resistance, making it popular for automotive and household products. Nickel-based plating provides a uniform and shiny finish, commonly used in electronics and consumer goods. Other Metal-based plating options include gold, silver, and copper, each offering unique aesthetic and functional properties for a variety of applications.</p></p>
