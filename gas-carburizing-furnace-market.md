<p><p>Gas carburizing furnaces are commonly available in two types - Roller Hearth Continuous Gas Carburizing Furnace and Push Plate Continuous Gas Carburizing Furnace. The Roller Hearth furnace utilizes a roller system to move the parts through the heating chamber continuously, while the Push Plate furnace uses a push plate mechanism to move parts along the chamber. Both types offer efficient and consistent gas carburizing processes for various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
