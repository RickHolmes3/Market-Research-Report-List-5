<p><p>Water-cooled brakes are braking systems that use water as a cooling medium to dissipate heat generated during braking. These brakes are commonly used in heavy-duty applications such as industrial machinery, cranes, elevators, and wind turbines, where high frictional heat is generated during braking. Water-cooled brakes offer superior heat dissipation capabilities compared to traditional air-cooled brakes, resulting in better brake performance and increased durability.</p><p>The water-cooled brake market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.5% during the forecast period.
