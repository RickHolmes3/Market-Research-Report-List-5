<p><p>Air release valves are crucial components in plumbing systems to release trapped air, ensuring optimal performance. There are four main types of air release valves - heating type, trace type, rapid type, and combined type. Heating type valves are suitable for systems where air accumulates slowly, trace type valves are used in systems with low or intermittent air accumulation, rapid type valves are designed for systems with high air accumulation, and combined type valves offer a combination of features for versatile applications. Each type caters to specific needs in plumbing systems.</p></p>
