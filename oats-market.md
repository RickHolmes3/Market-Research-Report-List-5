<p><p>The oats market can be categorized into three main types: groats, regular/flakes, and powder/flour. Regular or flakes oats are rolled or steel-cut, resulting in a chewy texture that is commonly used in oatmeal or granola. Oat powder or flour is made by grinding oats into a fine powder, which is often used in baking recipes as a gluten-free alternative.</p></p>
