Ni-Cd batteries are known for their durability and long-lasting charge, making them ideal for heavy use. Ni-MH batteries offer a higher capacity and are more environmentally friendly than Ni-Cd batteries. Li-ion batteries are the most lightweight and have a high energy density, providing long-lasting power for walkie talkies. Each type of battery caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
