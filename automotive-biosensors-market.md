<p><strong>The Automotive Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Biosensors</li><li>Piezo-Electric Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive biosensors are classified into different types based on their operating principles. Thermal biosensors use heat generated by biochemical reactions to detect analytes, while Piezo-electric biosensors convert mechanical stress into electrical signals. Thermal biosensors are preferred for their simplicity and fast response times, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid detection. On the other hand, Piezo-electric biosensors offer high sensitivity and can detect small changes in mass, making them ideal for precise measurements in automotive applications.</p></p>
