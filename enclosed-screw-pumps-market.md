<p><strong>The Enclosed Screw Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Multi-screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enclosed Screw Pumps are classified into two main types: Single Screw and Multi-screw pumps. Single screw pumps have one rotating screw that pushes fluid through the pump, making them ideal for low to medium flow rates. On the other hand, multi-screw pumps have multiple screws working together to move the fluid, allowing them to handle higher flow rates and pressures. Both types of pumps are commonly used in various industries for their efficiency, reliability, and versatility in fluid handling applications.</p></p>
