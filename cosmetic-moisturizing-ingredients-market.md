<p><p>Cosmetic Moisturizing Ingredients market consists of various types such as Polyols, Natural Moisturizing Factor (NMF), Amino Acids, and Molecular Biochemistry. Polyols are humectants that attract water to the skin, providing hydration. NMF are natural components of the skin that help maintain moisture levels. Amino Acids help improve skin elasticity and hydration. Molecular Biochemistry refers to ingredients that penetrate the skin at a cellular level to provide deep hydration. These different types cater to various skin needs and preferences in the cosmetic industry.</p></p>
