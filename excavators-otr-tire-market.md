<p><p>Excavators OTR Tire market consists of two main types: Radial Tire and Bias Tire. Radial tires have a ply running perpendicular to the direction of travel, allowing for greater stability and longer tread life. They are typically used for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Bias tires have multiple plies overlaid at an angle to the direction of travel, providing more flexibility and a smoother ride. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of excavator operators and buyers in the market.</p></p>
