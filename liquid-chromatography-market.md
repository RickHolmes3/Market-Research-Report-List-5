<p><p>Liquid chromatography market is segmented into different types based on the performance and pressure levels. High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) is a widely used technique for separating compounds in a mixture. Ultra High Pressure Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) offers higher resolution and faster analysis compared to HPLC. Low Pressure Liquid Chromatography (LPLC) is used for less demanding separations. Other markets include affinity chromatography, size exclusion chromatography, and ion exchange chromatography, which cater to specific analytical needs in various industries.</p></p>
