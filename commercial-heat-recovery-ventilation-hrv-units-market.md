<p><p>Commercial heat recovery ventilation (HRV) units come in different types such as rotary heat exchangers and plate heat exchangers. Rotary heat exchangers use a rotating wheel to transfer heat between outgoing and incoming air streams, while plate heat exchangers use a series of plates to achieve the same purpose. Both types of HRV units are designed to improve energy efficiency by recovering heat from exhaust air and transferring it to incoming fresh air, thus reducing the overall heating and cooling costs for commercial buildings.</p></p>
