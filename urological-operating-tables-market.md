<p><p>Urological operating tables are available in different types based on their functionality: Electric tables are operated using electrical mechanisms for height adjustment and positioning. Hydraulic tables use hydraulic fluid to control movements. Electro-hydraulic tables combine both electrical and hydraulic systems for precise positioning. Manual tables are operated manually without any electronic or hydraulic assistance. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different preferences and requirements of urologists and surgical teams.</p></p>
