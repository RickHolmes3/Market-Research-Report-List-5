<p><p>Industrial cyanoacrylate adhesives are classified into three main types based on their viscosity levels: low, medium, and high viscosity. Low viscosity adhesives are thin and runny, making them ideal for smooth surfaces and quick bonding. Medium viscosity adhesives offer a balance between flowability and gap filling capabilities. High viscosity adhesives are thick and provide strong bonding for rough or uneven surfaces. Each type caters to specific application requirements in industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
