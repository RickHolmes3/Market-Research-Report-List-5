<p><ul><li>Normally Open</li><li>Normally Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car relay market types include Normally Open and Normally Closed relays. Normally Open relays have contacts that are open when the relay is not energized, allowing current to flow only when the relay is activated. In contrast, Normally Closed relays have contacts that are closed when the relay is in its resting state, only opening to interrupt current when the relay is activated. Both types serve different functions in controlling electrical circuits in vehicles, offering versatility and customization options for various applications.</p></p>
