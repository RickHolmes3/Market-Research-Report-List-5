<p><p>De-NOx catalysts are types of catalysts used to reduce nitrogen oxide emissions from combustion processes. Two common types of De-NOx catalysts are honeycomb and flat catalysts. Honeycomb catalysts are structured in a honeycomb-like pattern, providing a large surface area for efficient catalytic reactions. Flat catalysts, on the other hand, consist of a flat surface coated with active materials for reducing nitrogen oxide emissions. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, power generation, and manufacturing to comply with environmental regulations and improve air quality.</p></p>
