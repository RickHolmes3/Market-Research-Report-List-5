<p><ul><li>Parallel Gripping System</li><li>Centric Gripping System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parallel Gripping Systems have two jaws that move in parallel to grip objects horizontally or vertically, providing stable and secure grip. They are commonly used in applications requiring a firm and precise grip, such as assembly lines and packaging. Centric Gripping Systems operate by gripping objects from the center, providing a balanced grip and ensuring equal force distribution. They are suitable for handling delicate objects or irregularly shaped items, commonly used in industries like electronics and food processing.</p></p>
