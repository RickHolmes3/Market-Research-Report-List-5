<p><p>MDPE material market can be categorized into two types based on the manufacturing processes used: Autoclave Process and Tubular Process. Autoclave Process involves using high pressure and temperature to create MDPE materials with high density and improved properties, making it suitable for applications requiring high performance. Tubular Process, on the other hand, involves extruding the material through a tubular die to create a more cost-effective solution for standard applications. Both processes cater to different market demands and offer a range of options for various applications.</p></p>
