<p><ul><li>Single-Axis Motion Controller</li><li>Multi-Axis Motion Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Servo controllers are categorized into two main types: single-axis motion controller and multi-axis motion controller. Single-axis motion controllers are designed to control one servo motor or actuator, making them suitable for applications requiring simple control over linear or rotational motion. On the other hand, multi-axis motion controllers can control multiple servo motors simultaneously, offering synchronized control over complex motion sequences in applications such as robotics, CNC machines, and automated manufacturing systems. Both types of servo controllers play a crucial role in enhancing precision and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
