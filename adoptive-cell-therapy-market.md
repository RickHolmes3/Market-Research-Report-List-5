<p><p>Adoptive Cell Therapy (ACT) is a promising approach in cancer treatment, with different types of therapies being developed such as CAR-T, TCR-T, NK, and TIL. CAR-T therapy involves genetically modifying T cells to recognize and attack cancer cells. TCR-T therapy utilizes T cell receptors to target specific antigens on cancer cells. NK therapy harnesses the natural killer cells of the immune system to fight cancer. TIL therapy involves harvesting tumor-infiltrating lymphocytes and enhancing their anti-tumor activity before reinfusion into the patient.</p></p>
