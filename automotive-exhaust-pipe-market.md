<p><p>The automotive exhaust pipe market consists of various types to meet the needs of different vehicle models and performance levels. Back pressure pipe is designed to create a higher level of back pressure, while half back pressure pipe offers a moderate level. Straight pipe allows for maximum exhaust flow, while half straight pipe strikes a balance between flow and back pressure.
