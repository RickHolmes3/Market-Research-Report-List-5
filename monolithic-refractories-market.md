<p><p>Monolithic refractories refer to a type of refractory material that is used in a variety of industrial applications. There are three main types of monolithic refractories in the market: castables, ramming masses, and others. Castables are refractory materials that can be mixed with water and molded into complex shapes. Ramming masses are used for lining large industrial furnaces and are typically made of high-quality grains and powders. The "others" category includes a wide range of monolithic refractory products such as plastic refractories, gunning mixes, and mortars.</p></p>
