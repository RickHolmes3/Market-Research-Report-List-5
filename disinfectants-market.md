<p><strong>The Disinfectants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidising Disinfectants</li><li>Non-oxidising Disinfectants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disinfectants are categorized into two main types: oxidizing disinfectants and non-oxidizing disinfectants. Oxidizing disinfectants work by releasing oxygen atoms that destroy bacteria and viruses. Examples include chlorine bleach and hydrogen peroxide. Non-oxidizing disinfectants work by disrupting the cell membranes of bacteria and viruses, causing them to die. Examples include quaternary ammonium compounds and phenolic compounds. Both types are commonly used in various settings such as hospitals, households, and industries to prevent the spread of infectious diseases.</p></p>
