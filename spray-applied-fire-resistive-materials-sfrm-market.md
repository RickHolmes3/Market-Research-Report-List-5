There are two main types of SFRM: Cellulosic SFRM, which is made from cellulose fibers and provides fire resistance by creating a layer of char when exposed to high temperatures, and Hydrocarbon SFRM, which is made from mineral fibers and provides protection through the release of water vapor when heated. Both types of SFRM play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and integrity of buildings in the event of a fire.</p></p>
