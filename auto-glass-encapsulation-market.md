<p><p>Auto glass encapsulation refers to the process of sealing the edges of automotive glass with a material to protect it from external elements. There are various types of materials used for this purpose, including Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE), Polyurethane (PUR), Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM), and Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC). TPE offers flexibility and durability, PUR provides strong adhesion, EPDM has excellent weather resistance, and PVC offers cost-effectiveness. Each material has specific properties that cater to different needs in the auto glass encapsulation market.</p></p>
