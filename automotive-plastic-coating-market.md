<p><strong>The Automotive Plastic Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Base Coat</li><li>Clearcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive plastic coating market consists of three main types: Primer, Base Coat, and Clearcoat. Primer is applied first to ensure adhesion between the plastic surface and subsequent coatings. Base Coat provides color and enhances the visual appeal of the vehicle. Clearcoat is the final layer that adds gloss and protects the underlying layers from damage and UV exposure. Each type plays a crucial role in the overall durability and appearance of the plastic coating used in automotive applications.</p></p>
