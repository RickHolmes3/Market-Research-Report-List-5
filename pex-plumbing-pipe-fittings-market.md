<p><p>PEX plumbing pipes and fittings come in three different types: PEX-A, PEX-B, and PEX-C. PEX-A is known for its superior flexibility and shape memory, allowing for easy installation even in tight spaces. PEX-B is less flexible but more affordable, making it a popular choice for residential plumbing projects. PEX-C falls in between, offering a balance of flexibility and cost-effectiveness. All three types offer excellent resistance to corrosion and scale buildup, making them durable and long-lasting choices for plumbing systems.</p></p>
