<p><strong>The Digital Fabric Printing Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reactive dye inks are used for printing on natural fibers like cotton and linen, providing vibrant and long-lasting colors. Acidic inks are designed for printing on protein-based fibers like silk and wool. Paint inks are thicker and used for printing on a variety of fabrics, providing a textured finish. Dispersion inks are water-based inks suitable for printing on synthetic fabrics. Sublimation inks are used for printing on polyester fabrics, where the ink turns into a gas under heat and bonds with the fabric for a permanent print.</p></p>
