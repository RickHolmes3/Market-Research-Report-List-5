<p><p>Paint-Coated Steel Strapping is available in two main types - Oscillated Wound Coil and Ribbon Wound Coil markets. Oscillated Wound Coil steel strapping is wound in a tight, compact spiral, allowing for longer footage per coil and less coil changes. This type is commonly used in high volume applications. Ribbon Wound Coil steel strapping is wound like a ribbon, allowing for easy dispensing and tangle-free usage. This type is ideal for smaller operations or portable applications.</p></p>
