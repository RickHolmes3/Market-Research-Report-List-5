Chemical indicators are synthetic compounds that change color in the presence of calcium ions, making them useful for visual detection. Genetically encoded indicators are proteins that fluoresce when bound to calcium ions, allowing for real-time imaging of calcium levels in living cells. Both types of indicators are important tools in research and diagnostics, providing valuable insight into cellular activities and signaling pathways.</p></p>
