<p><strong>The Steel Refractory Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brick Refractories</li><li>Monolithic Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel refractory material market includes two main types: brick refractories and monolithic refractories. Brick refractories are made from individual bricks that are assembled to form walls or linings. They are commonly used in steel production due to their high resistance to heat and mechanical stress. Monolithic refractories, on the other hand, are in a ready-to-use form and can be poured or sprayed onto surfaces. They offer easier installation and better resistance to thermal shock, making them a popular choice in the steel industry.</p></p>
