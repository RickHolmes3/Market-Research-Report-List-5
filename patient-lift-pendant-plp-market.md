<p><p>Patient Lift Pendant (PLP) market offers two main types: Single-Mast and Twin-Mast. Single-Mast patient lifts consist of a single vertical mast attached to a ceiling track, providing a stable and secure lifting solution for patients. On the other hand, Twin-Mast patient lifts feature two vertical masts for increased stability and weight capacity, making them suitable for heavier patients or those requiring additional support. Both options offer safe and efficient lifting solutions for healthcare facilities and caregivers.</p></p>
