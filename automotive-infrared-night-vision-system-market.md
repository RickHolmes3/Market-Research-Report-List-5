<p><p>Automotive Infrared Night Vision System market is divided into two main types: Active Infrared Night Vision System and Passive Infrared Night Vision System. Active systems use infrared light to illuminate the road ahead, while passive systems rely on thermal imaging to detect heat emitted by objects. Active systems are more common in luxury vehicles as they provide clearer images, while passive systems are preferred for their ability to detect objects in complete darkness. Both systems enhance driver visibility and ultimately contribute to safer driving experiences.</p></p>
