<p><ul><li>Multi-leaf Springs</li><li>Mono-leaf Springs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile sheet spring assemblies come in two main types: Multi-leaf springs and Mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple layers of spring steel bound together to provide greater flexibility and load-bearing capacity. They are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles such as trucks and buses. On the other hand, Mono-leaf springs are made of a single piece of spring steel and are lighter and more compact, making them suitable for smaller vehicles like sedans and compact cars. Both types cater to different needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
