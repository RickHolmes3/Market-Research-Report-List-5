<p><p>Mass flow meters for the semiconductor market come in two main types: liquid mass flow meters and gas mass flow meters. Liquid mass flow meters are designed to accurately measure the flow rate of liquids within semiconductor processing equipment. Gas mass flow meters, on the other hand, are used to measure the flow rate of gases, such as nitrogen or argon, which are often utilized in the semiconductor manufacturing process. Both types of mass flow meters play a critical role in ensuring precise control and monitoring of fluid flows in semiconductor production.</p></p>
