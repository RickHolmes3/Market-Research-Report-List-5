<p><strong>The Chemical Accelerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Accelerators</li><li>Secondary Accelerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Primary accelerators are chemicals that are added in small quantities to rubber compounds to increase the speed of the vulcanization process. They are typically sulfur-bearing compounds that work in conjunction with sulfur to cross-link rubber molecules. Secondary accelerators, on the other hand, are added in larger quantities and work in tandem with primary accelerators to further enhance the vulcanization process. They help improve the overall performance and properties of the rubber material. Both types of accelerators play a crucial role in rubber manufacturing.</p></p>
