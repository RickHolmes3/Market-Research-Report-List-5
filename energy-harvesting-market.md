<p><p>Energy harvesting is a growing market that includes various types such as photovoltaic, thermoelectric, piezoelectric, and electrodynamic technologies. Photovoltaic energy harvesting converts sunlight into electricity, while thermoelectric harvesting generates power from temperature differences. Piezoelectric harvesting uses vibrations or mechanical stress to produce electricity, and electrodynamic harvesting captures energy from motion or magnetic fields. These different types of energy harvesting technologies cater to diverse applications and offer sustainable solutions for powering various devices and systems.</p></p>
