<p><p>The Electromagnetic Proportional Valve market can be categorized into two main types: Direct-acting Type and Pilot-operated Type valves. Direct-acting Type valves are designed to respond directly to changes in the input signal, providing accurate and fast control of fluid flow. On the other hand, Pilot-operated Type valves use a pilot valve to control the main valve, allowing for higher flow rates and better overall efficiency. Both types of valves play a crucial role in various industrial applications that require precise control of fluid flow.</p></p>
