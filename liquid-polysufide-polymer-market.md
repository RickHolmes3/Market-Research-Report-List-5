<p><ul><li>Thiol End</li><li>Epoxy End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid polysulfide polymer market can be categorized into two main types based on the end groups present in the polymer chains - Thiol End and Epoxy End. Thiol End polymers contain sulfur atoms with thiol (sulfhydryl) end groups, making them highly reactive towards other chemical compounds. On the other hand, Epoxy End polymers have epoxy (oxirane) end groups, which give them excellent adhesive properties. Both types of liquid polysulfide polymers find applications in various industries such as construction, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
