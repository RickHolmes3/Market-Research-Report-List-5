<p><ul><li>Batch Furnace</li><li>Continuous Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal Infusion Model Processing Market includes two main types: Batch Furnace and Continuous Furnace. Batch Furnace is a type of processing where a fixed amount of metal is placed in a furnace, heated, and then removed once the process is complete. This method is suitable for small-scale production. Continuous Furnace, on the other hand, is a process where metal is continuously fed into the furnace, melted, and then poured into molds. This method is more suitable for large-scale production and offers higher efficiency.</p></p>
