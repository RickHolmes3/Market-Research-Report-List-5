<p><p>R134A Refrigerant is produced using two main processes: Tetrachlorethylene Process and Trichlorethylene Process. The Tetrachlorethylene Process involves the reaction of tetrachloroethene with hydrogen fluoride to produce R134A. On the other hand, the Trichlorethylene Process involves the reaction of trichloroethylene with hydrogen fluoride to form R134A. Both processes are used in the market for producing R134A Refrigerant, catering to different requirements and applications in the refrigeration and air conditioning industries.</p></p>
